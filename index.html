This list is kept and updated by the 2builders12rules team. This is NOT the entire list or the official list. This is just what we have compiled from what people have reported in our discord (linked at the top). This means that these lists may also not be completely accurate but we do our best to verify the lists and keep them as up to date as possible.

To report a punishment, join our discord and let us know your Minecraft username, your punishment, and the reason if you know it.

This is a list of KNOWN punishments on 2b2t.
Some punishments listed below can be temporary (days, months and sometimes years), and in rare cases, some can be permanent. Prio stripping is an exception and is immediate.

Mute: Unable to send chat messages. Also known as a "Soft Mute".
PM Mute: Unable to send private messages with commands like /msg, /w, /l, /t or any other commands that allow you to send private messages. Also known as a "Whisper Mute".
Hard Mute: Mute & PM Mute. Unable to communicate through server chat at all.
Prio Strip: Priority queue is removed before it should be removed (before the month is over).
Prio Ban: Priority queue is removed and you are unable to buy it again.
Blacklist: All blacklisted accounts get kicked from queue or game regularly (aka shadow banning).
IP Ban: If a player is IP Banned, any accounts they log into will be prio banned, hard muted, and/or added to the blacklist.
Paypal Ban: Similar to IP Ban but your paypal purchase history on 2b2t webstore is used instead to prio ban every account you have ever bought prio for.
Hard Ban: Unable to join the server/queue at all.
Death Mute: Kills and Deaths that involve you will no longer send to chat.
Sign Mute: You can no longer write on signs.
